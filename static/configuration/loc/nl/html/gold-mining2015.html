Goldmining 2015

Build upon the existing method and network of regional experts during the REDD+ for the Guiana Shield project, WWF proposed to formalize a participatory observatory of the pressures on the environment (OPPEN). The focus is on the impact of gold mining activities with the aim to consolidate the transnational cooperation and to produce shared figures on a yearly basis. Therefor the Gold mining shape file for 2015 was produced by FCMU.
