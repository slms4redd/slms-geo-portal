Deforestation 2000 - 2019

The deforestation maps for 2000-2017 were produced by the Forest Cover Monitoring Unit with support of the ACTO-project “Monitoring the forest cover in the Amazon region”.

The maps were produced with Landsat images, using following softwares: TerraAmazon, PostGreSQL, QGIS for the years 2009, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017 and 2019.

An accuracy assessment voor 2000-2009 and 2009-2015 was carried out in collaboration with the UN-REDD program.
