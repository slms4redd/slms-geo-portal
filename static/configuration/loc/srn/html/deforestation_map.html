Busi di den puru 2000 - 2017

A karta disi e sori den presi pe a busi puru foe 2009, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016 nanga 2017 FCMU meki na karta disi nanga kraka foe a ACTO-project “Monitoring the forest cover in the Amazon region”.

Den karta dis meki nanga landsat prenki en den gebroiki wan computer systema kari TerraAmazon, PostgreSQL nanga QGIS gi den jari 2009, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016 nanga 2017.
